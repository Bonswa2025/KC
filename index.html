About Us

Tired of unreliable pugs? Looking for a real team to push higher keys with? Keystone Crushers is a dedicated Mythic+ community of experienced World of Warcraft players focused on Keystone Level 12 and beyond. We’re more than just a Discord server – we’re a competitive, supportive, and social group that thrives on teamwork, strategy, and the thrill of the timer. Whether you’re aiming to refine your skills or smash your next record, you’ll find your crew here. Visit us at Raider.IO

What We Offer

  • Active community pushing +12 keys and higher
  • Focus on quality runs and consistent improvement
  • Supportive players who share strategies, tips, and laughs
  • Forge lasting friendships in and out of dungeons
  • If you’re an ambitious Mythic+ enthusiast who lives for the challenge, Keystone Crushers is the place to be.

Who We're Looking For

We're recruiting committed players who know their class, show up consistently, and want to grow with us. Whether you're a tank, healer, or DPS — if you've got the right mindset, we want you!
