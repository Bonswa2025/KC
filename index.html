About Us

Tired of unreliable pugs? Looking for a real team to push higher keys with?

Keystone Crushers is a dedicated Mythic+ community of experienced World of Warcraft players focused on Keystone Level 12 and beyond.

We’re more than just a Discord server – we’re a competitive, supportive, and social group that thrives on teamwork, strategy, and the thrill of the timer.

Whether you’re aiming to refine your skills or smash your next record, you’ll find your crew here.

What We Offer

  • A supportive, competitive, and social community focused on high-level Mythic+ content


  • Experienced players with 3000+ Raider.IO scores


  • Clean and focused voice comms on Discord with active voice chats


  • Non-toxic environment where teamwork and growth come first


  • Party Crusher System (coming soon) – a quick-match tool to find key groups fast within the community


Who We're Looking For

We’re recruiting committed, experienced players who want to grow with a like-minded Mythic+ team.
